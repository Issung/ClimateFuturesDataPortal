Knowing the user interface

Introduction of the interface of the application.

  • Area Selection

    • This area contains the section where you can proceed your location selection for data extraction. It contains a map API from ArcGIS and functionality the team implements to help you choose area of your choice, by marking with square.



  • Time & Variable Selection

    • After having chosen your desired location for data extraction, you can then proceed to choose time period and variables. Time period contains a timespan of 2 checkpoint. There are over a hundred variables to choose from so you can use the search functionality to search for your desired variables. Time resolution is where the climate futures data is collected by real world stations, and they may record data every hour, every day, every month etc,



  • Summary & Download

    • This area contains the information about your downloads. It sums up your chosen area by geographical degree and the timespan. even if you select a specific time resolution before procceding to download option you will still have access to other time resolution tabs but it will automatically select the tab that you have chose before.
