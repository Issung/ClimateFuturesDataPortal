The Interface (Steps)

This section will introduce to the overall functionalities and different purposes of the various interface components.

Steps Selection

The data portal interface is broken up into separate "steps". These interfaces can be accessed by clicking the desired steps tabs at the navigation bar: Area, Time & Variables, Summary and Manual across the top of the screen. The Manual tab will open the user manual in a new tab so that you do not lose your current filter selections.

Previous Step, Next Step and Download buttons

Another way to navigate through the program is the Previous Step and Next Step buttons. These buttons will allow you to go backwards and forwards through the process just as the navigation bar tabs do, it is up to your preference which you wish to use.

Note: These buttons are only displayed if your screen has enough space.

At the final Summary step, the Next Step button will be replaced by a Search Data button. After pressing this button, the application will proceed to searching for climate data within your set filters.
