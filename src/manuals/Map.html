Area

In this section, you will be able to choose the area of choice to extract geographical data from. The area of choice can be selected by drawing a bounding box (with rectangular shape). This bounding box will wrap around the area of your choice, including the numeric value of longtitude and latitude.

This window area contains the interactive map that illustrates the surface of the earth, looking from satelite. The map is provided by the arcGIS map API. Below is an overview of the interactive map window.

Places of interest

This section will outline the specific details of important signs, texts and features that you should pay attention to whilst using the application in this first step.

  • Toolbox

    This box contains all of the actions you can take when using the interactive map. The contents of the box are as followed:

    • Transform: switching between drawing bounding box and moving the selected box around.
    • Draw rentangle: drawing the rectangular bounding box.
    • Undo: reversing to your previous action.
    • Redo: restoring any actions that were previously undone using an undo.
    • Delete: deleting current selected bounding box.
    • Status box: current number of bounding box.

  • Longtitude and latitude

    In this example, the longtitude is -171.868 and the latitude is -44.127

  • Zooming buttons

    You can zoom in the application by pressing the "+" button, and zoom out by pressing the "-" button

How to draw the bounding box

This section will guide towards how to draw the bounding box which will contain the coordination to extract data from the map.

  • Step 1 - Choose the Draw Rectangle option

  • Step 2 - Hold your left mouse button, drag and drop to draw a rectangular shape. This shape will contain your desired location for extraction.
    In this example, Tasmania has been selected.

  • Step 3 (Extra) - The following are addtional actions you can take to customise your previously drawn rectangle

    • Dragging and dropping your drawn rectangle to other places on the map

    • Undoing and redoing your actions

    • Deleting selected bounding box
